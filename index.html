About Me

I'm Cindy Wu...

Cindy Wu was born and raised in Las Vegas, NV. Hard work has been an attribute she's held as a young girl. She grew up working in her family's restaurant to support her family along the way. During that time she's admired service, even giving an appreciation for our military as a child.

Currently at this time, Cindy works at Caesars Palace on the Las Vegas strip helping to give tourists a friendly experience when visiting the hotel. While also doing modeling gigs, Cindy empowers other women through participation in fitness boot camps that allow them to gain valuable connections with others while improving their physical and mental fitness.

When not involved with these other pursuits, she's consistently out every Thursday morning giving back to the community with food drives, assisting the Hope for the City church organization. Cindy now wants to serve her community as the next Miss Nevada. She plans on using pageantry to build a bigger platform to bring awareness to the increasing prevalence of loneliness happening in Las Vegas, the state of Nevada, and across our nation.
